Bridging time and place

Apart from the very aesthetically designed ambience, we also host an art gallery that is on par with international norms.

The majority of the walls have exposed bricks, wooden beams support the traditional tiled roofing and the courtyard possesses certain magic in the air.

Serene art gallery in Kozhikode exhibiting artworks by local & international artists.

Gudhaam is filled with unexpected pieces of art everywhere you look – antique door hinges, traditional palace fittings, traditional Malabar architecture designs and unique furniture. There is also an array of quirky art pieces collected from several countries over the years. At the very centre of Calicut’s historic art and commercial hub, Gudhaam sets the stage for collaborations between artists, professionals, students and art lovers alike. With an experienced in-house curator and professional artist management services, Gudhaam is at the forefront of creating a Calicut creative collective.

A stage to honour homegrown artists

Gudhaam seeks to pay homage to genuine artists of various mediums from around the world.
